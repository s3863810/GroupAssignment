I was born in Mildura Victoria and moved here to Darwin Northern Territory 20 years ago. The weather here is much different than Mildura. Mildura has four seasons, autumn, winter, spring, summer; here in Darwin there are only two seasons, wet or dry. During the wet season we can receive up to 2 metres of rain over the six months.

When I was 23 years old, I lived and worked on a farm outside Rome Italy, in a town called Albano Lazziale. I attended night school language classes there and learnt to speak a small amount of Italian. The class had people from all around Europe, not one of us could speak the same language, the only way for us to communicate to each other was to speak in Italian. Do you remember the old comedy TV show “Welcome back Kotter” with John Travolta? Their class was made up from students with different nationalities and most of them couldn’t speak English. That is how I compared our Italian language classes.
